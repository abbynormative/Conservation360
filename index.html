Project Synopsis

Seneca Park Zoo Society is the nonprofit partner to Monroe Countyâ€™s Seneca Park Zoo. The Society is a multifaceted organization that partners with conservation organizations internationally to help preserve endangered species, educate the public about conservation programs and their impact.

As the society continues to sponsor conservation efforts, they have a growing need for technology that is able to aggregate and display the impact their work has to researchers, conservationists, and the general public. Conservation organizations lack the proper infrastructure and ability to centralize and disseminate conservation data efficiently and at scale.

In order to help alleviate these issues, this project aims to provide a web portal that allows donors and researchers to access datasets, aggregate information, and use geospatial and temporal attributes to analyze related datasets.

Building off of the architectural and technological design of a previous senior project team, our team will strive to provide the Seneca Park Zoo Society, as well as conservation groups and the public at large, with the ability to conveniently view the impact of conservation efforts. The team hopes to achieve this using Salesforce technologies connected to a PostGIS database that can be accessed from a web portal or queried via an API.
